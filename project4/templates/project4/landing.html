Welcome

This mini-study explores how a recommender learns your taste when you’re brand new. As you rate a few movies, the interface may show a small “what-if” preview—how a low vs. high rating could change your future suggestions. The goal is to see whether this kind of transparency helps people get better recommendations, faster.
